## Raw Data for GHG Emissions across Canada's Provinces and Territories.

* Source link for raw data:

* https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/t1/tbl1/en/tv.action?pid=3810009701&cubeTimeFrame.startYear=2013&cubeTimeFrame.endYear=2023&referencePeriods=20130101%2C20230101 *

### The raw GHG emissions dataset was sourced from the Statistics Canada/Government of Canada Open Data portal and contains historical greenhouse gas emissions data across Canadian provinces and sectors. 

* Emissions are measured in kilotonnes of carbon dioxide equivalent (kt CO₂e).

### The dataset (14k+ rows) includes:

* Provincial and national emissions records
* Sector categories such as transportation, 
* electricity, 
* manufacturing, 
* mining,
* households, and
* agriculture
* Historical yearly emissions data for trend analysis

The raw data also contained unnecessary metadata columns, duplicate records, coded sector labels, and reconciliation rows that required cleaning before analysis.

The dataset serves as the foundation for emissions trend analysis, province comparisons, and forecasting.
